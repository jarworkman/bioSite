About Kirsten

Clarkson College and Career

Kirsten is a full-time student nursing major at Carkson College, going for her RN/BSN and aspires to eventually go on after a year in the field to get her Nurse Practioner certification. She is also a full-time employee at Children's Hospital here in Omaha in the NICU as a CNA. She has found that she loves working with babies and wants to continue working in the NICU when she eventually becomes a nurse. Fortunately, as opposed to many other CNA positions, she is allowed a growing scope to keep learning and advancing, regardless of title.

Life and Traveling

Kirsten has lived most of her life here in Nebraska but in high school moved to Georgia and then Florida because her Mom joined the National Guard. Although a difficult experience for her living and going to the same school her whole life, she will also tell you that it was on of the best things for her. She really got to see things that she was never exposed to before and showed her there's more of a world than the small town she lived in. She realized that she has an incredible love for the ocean, and traveling in general. With such a love for the ocean, and just nature in general, she's spent the last couple of years budgeting for different "vacations", but they are mostly exploration missions to find the one place in the country she really wants to reside. She's been to both coasts, drove through many other states, and been to the Colorado Rockys so far, with Charleston, SC up next in a couple of weeks.
